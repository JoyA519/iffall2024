Tuwon Madara

Milk Candy

Contains: Dairy
Research Evualuation

Tuwon Madara, also known as milk candy, is a typically 3 ingredient recipe made in Northern Nigeria. The recipe involves boiling the ingredients into a thick paste to then be cooled and shaped. You can make all sorts of cute and fun designs using cookie cutters or sculpting the paste with your hand.

Ingredients

  • 5 Cups Powdered Milk
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 2/3 Cup Water
  • Vanilla Extract
  • food colour of choice
  • cookie cutter

Instructions (need images alongside instructions)

  1. In a pot add water, sugar, butter and vanilla extract(you can add food colour in this step if you like), allow it to boil, keep stirring until the sugar completely melt then add your powdered milk and stir.
  2. Turn off the heat and keep mixing until well combine, bring leather and rub it with some butter then transfer the milk candy
  3. Grease your palms with butter, take the portion of the milk candy and mould it to foam a ball, repeat till all are finished and allow it to cool
  4. Shape it to your desire shape, I used mamoul cutter to shape mine. Enjoooy
